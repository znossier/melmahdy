# Image Setup Guide

Place all images in the following locations:

## Avatar
- **Memoji**: `/public/memoji.png`

## Company Logos
Place in `/public/logos/`:
- `spiralyze.png` - Spiralyze logo (white S shape on blue)
- `valu.png` - valU logo (teal "valu*" with orange asterisk)
- `auc.png` - The American University in Cairo logo (white geometric design on dark blue)
- `aaib.png` - Arab African International Bank logo (gold/bronze emblem)
- `mepi.png` - MEPI/Tomorrow's Leaders logo (US State Department seal with MEPI text)
- `isee.png` - The International School of Elite Education logo (circular blue band with book)

## Tech Stack Logos
Place in `/public/tech-stack/`:
- `python.png` - Python logo (blue and yellow interlocking snakes)
- `hadoop.png` - Apache Hadoop logo (yellow elephant)
- `h2o.png` - H2O.ai logo (yellow square with "H2O.ai" text)
- `cpp.png` - C++ logo (blue hexagon with white "C++")
- `spark.png` - Apache Spark logo (orange star with "Apache Spark" text)
- `tensorflow.png` - TensorFlow logo (orange gradient "TF" letters)
- `keras.png` - Keras logo (red square with white "K")
- `sql.png` - SQL logo (orange database icon with "SQL" text)
- `scikit-learn.png` - scikit-learn logo (blue and orange organic shapes)
- `powerbi.png` - Power BI logo (if you have it, otherwise will use placeholder)

## Notes
- All images should be PNG format
- Recommended size: 200x200px for logos, 300x300px for Memoji
- The components will automatically fall back to styled placeholders if images are missing
- Images will be optimized by Next.js automatically
